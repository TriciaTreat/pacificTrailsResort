Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it
alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are avialable for guest use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watchingat Pacific Trails, we offer giuded bird-
watching trips at sunrise several times a week.
